# Season 5 Weekly Predictions

Progressive Monte Carlo simulation predictions for each week of Season 5.

**IMPORTANT NOTE:** These predictions are based on strength score analysis and statistical modeling. The actual judges may disagree with these assessments, as they consider factors beyond our scoring system. Only one contestant can be Star Baker per week, and the final round determines the overall series winner (not a weekly Star Baker). **ACTUAL WINNER:** Sophie won Series 5.

**Method:** Monte Carlo simulation (10,000 iterations per week)

**Variance Calculation:**
- Week 1: All contestants use fixed variance (3.0)
- Week 2+: Variance calculated from contestant's performance up to that week
